Controls:

Q,E,W,S,A,D,U,O,I,K,J,L -- Control screen rotation

F -- move snake head x-
H -- move snake head x+
T -- move snake head y+
B -- move snake head y-
Y -- move snake head z+
V -- move snake head z-
R -- move snake head w+
N -- move snake head w-

G -- continue on path of last move

Axis lines will be at the head of the snake moving from current position to one coordinate forward on the respective axis;

Red -- x
Green -- y
Blue -- z
Purple -- w

Mouse controls can be changed by pressing 1, 2, 3, or 0
